# BIS Global Debt Securities Dashboard (1962–2025)

A multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing global debt securities data sourced from the Bank for International Settlements (BIS). This project covers over six decades of debt trends across 152 countries, examining regional distribution, growth rates, crisis periods, and market structure.

# Dashboard Pages

# Page 1 — Global Debt Overview
High-level summary of total global debt securities, country and regional distribution, and debt trends by decade. Key visuals include a decade trend line chart, top countries by total debt, regional donut chart, and debt by growth regime.

# Page 2 — Growth Rate Analysis
Quarter-over-quarter and year-over-year growth rate analysis across 152 countries from 1962 to 2025. Includes YoY growth rate over time, average growth rate by 
market regime, top countries by YoY performance, and regional YoY breakdown.

# Page 3 — Crisis & Market Structure
Examination of debt behavior before, during, and after major financial crises including the 2008 Global Financial Crisis and COVID-19 pandemic. Covers debt by issuer sector, debt by economic era, and a rolling average trend comparison.

# Key Metrics

Total Global Debt: $12bn (sum of value_millions_usd)
Countries Covered: 152
Date Range: 1962 to 2025
Latest Quarter: 2025-Q4
Total All-Time Highs: 107K
